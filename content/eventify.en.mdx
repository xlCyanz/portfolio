Hello everyone! Today I want to present a project I've been working on that solves one of the most tedious problems when organizing any type of activity: **Eventify**.

If you have ever organized a workshop, conference, or course, you know that post-event administration consumes a lot of time. Especially, the task of generating and sending attendance certificates one by one to each participant.

## What is Eventify?

**Eventify** is a comprehensive event management platform that not only helps you manage attendees but also automates the entire flow related to issuing certificates of participation from start to finish.

### Core Features

#### 1. Comprehensive Event Management 📅
From an intuitive dashboard, you can create and organize multiple events. Keeping a clear record of when they are held and who is registered.

#### 2. Automatic Certificate Issuance 🎓
Forget about using manual templates and editing names one by one. Eventify takes the participants' data and dynamically and automatically generates a personalized certificate for each person who has attended and completed the event.

#### 3. Automatic Email Delivery ✉️
Once the event concludes and the certificates are generated, the system takes care of everything. Eventify automatically sends emails to each participant's inbox, attaching their corresponding certificate.
