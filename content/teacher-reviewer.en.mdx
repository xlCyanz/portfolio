Hello everyone! Today I want to share a very special project called **Teacher Reviewer**, a platform I worked on during my studies.

If you have ever felt lost when selecting your courses because you didn't know the professors, you know how important it is to have good references. Inspired by this need, a group of friends and I decided to build a solution.

## What is Teacher Reviewer?

**Teacher Reviewer** is an application designed specifically for college students to **rate their teachers**. The main goal is to provide a real reference, based on peers' experiences, to help students make better choices during course selection.

### Core Features

#### 1. Authentic Reviews 📝
Students can leave comments about each professor's methodology, level of difficulty, and overall class quality.

#### 2. Numerical Ratings ⭐
Make decisions easier with a scoring system covering aspects like clarity in explanations and fairness in grading.

#### 3. Student-Oriented 🎓
Made by students, for students. The entire focus of the platform is to make the enrollment process less stressful and more informed.
